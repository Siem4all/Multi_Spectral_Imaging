# Biomedical Multispectral Imaging System

The biomedical multispectral imaging system includes an MSC2-BIO-1-A multispectral camera with 4 bands suitable for detecting hemoglobin and lipid in research applications.

The LED illuminator is equipped with 4 independent channels that are matched to the MSC2-BIO-1-A camera: 
- **735 nm**: sensitive to deoxyhemoglobin (deoxygenated blood).
- **800 nm**: sensitive to total hemoglobin (total blood).
- **865 nm**: sensitive to oxyhemoglobin (oxygenated blood).
- **930 nm**: sensitive to lipid.

With these four spectral images, a number of analyses can be performed, including oxygen saturation and total hemoglobin, which are important for monitoring tissue health. 

> **Note**: The MSIS-BIO-1-A is for investigational purposes only.
